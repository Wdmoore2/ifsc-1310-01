Dragon Vehicle Overview

Dragon is a spacecraft designed to deliver cargo and people to orbiting destinations. It made history in 2012 as the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to the ISS and safely return. Dragon carries cargo to space and is being refined for crew missions under NASA's agreement.

Configurations

Cargo - Resupply the Space Station

  • Delivery and Return: Dragon is the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to the ISS and return significant amounts of cargo to Earth.
  • Versatile Cargo Racks: Designed for efficient packing in zero gravity.

Crew - Taking Humans into Space

  • Partnership with NASA: Upgrades to allow for crew carrying capability.
  • Crew Upgrades: Seating for seven with life-support systems and a launch escape system.

Dragon Lab - A Free-Flying Microgravity Laboratory

  • Facilitating Research: Designed for research in a microgravity environment.
  • Longevity: Can transport experiments and instruments into space and back to Earth.
